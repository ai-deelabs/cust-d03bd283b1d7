Our studio

A great booth isn't equipment in a corner. It's a gathering place — where strangers become a conga line and the shyest guest finally lets loose.

Bless Studio Production started with a single open-air rig and a stubborn belief: the photo booth should be the most fun ten feet of any room. Today we build, light and staff every setup ourselves.

No vending-machine kiosks. No anonymous drop-offs. A real attendant arrives early, dials in the lighting for your venue, and keeps the line moving and the props flying all night long.

You get the prints, the digital gallery, and a room full of people who can't stop smiling. We handle everything in between.
